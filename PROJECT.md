# Projects Portfolio

This portfolio showcases enterprise analytics solutions and academic projects across Business Intelligence, Customer Analytics, Data Engineering, and Machine Learning.

The enterprise projects are based on real-world business problems in Pharmacy & Healthcare E-Commerce, while the academic projects demonstrate analytical methodologies and technical foundations developed during graduate studies.

# Enterprise Analytics Projects

| Project | Business Domain | Technology | Business Impact |

| Customer Service Analytics | Customer Experience | SQL, Power BI | 99% SLA |
